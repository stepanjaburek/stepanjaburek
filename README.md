#  PhD Student in Political Science (IR) at Charles University, Prague

## Currently mainly working on 3 research agendas connecting Political Science and NLP

  1. In my dissertation I research how domestic political incentives (electoral competition, government-opposition dynamics, etc.) shape partisan and elite communication about international economic cooperation (and global economic governance)
  2.  How authoritarian legacies affect ideological debates - specifically, whether “left” and “right” carry (a)symmetric connotations across European political context (with my co-authors).
  3.  How can open-source machine translation and open-source LLMs be leveraged in large-scale comparative, cross-national political research (with my co-authors)
