#  PhD Student in Political Science (IR) at Charles University, Prague

## Currently mainly working on 3 research agendas connecting Political Science and NLP

  1. In my dissertation I research how domestic political incentives (electoral competition, government-opposition dynamics, etc.) shape how political parties and elites communicate about international ecenomic cooperation (and global economic governance)
  2.  With my co-authors I research how authoritarian legacies affect ideological debates - specifically, whether “left” and “right” carry (a)symmetric connotations across European political contexts.
  3.  With my co-authors I research how can open-source machine translation and open-source LLMs be used in large-scale comparative political research,
